Insurance Cost Prediction Model

Overview

Developed and optimized a predictive model for estimating insurance costs using a variety of machine learning algorithms. This project was focused on understanding and applying different techniques to enhance model performance and ensure accuracy.

Responsibilities:

  • Data Preparation: Engaged in comprehensive data preprocessing, including handling missing values, feature engineering, and normalization to prepare the dataset for model training. This learning phase emphasized the importance of clean and well-prepared data for achieving accurate predictions.

  • Model Optimization: Applied hyperparameter tuning and cross-validation techniques to enhance model performance. Gained practical insights into optimizing model parameters and evaluating the impact of different settings on prediction accuracy.

  • Accuracy Assessment: Conducted thorough accuracy assessments, including metrics such as Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE), and R-squared, to evaluate the reliability and robustness of the model.
